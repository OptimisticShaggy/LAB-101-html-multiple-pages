The Expanse

Factions

There are three major factions throughout the galaxy; United Nations (UN), Martian Congretional Republic (MCR), and Outer Republic Alliance (OPA). UN was the first faction from Earth. MCR was formed by many others that had different views from how the UN did everything, with the goal to terriform and make a new home in Mars. The OPA is not seen as even a faction by the UN and MCR, more like a terroist organization. OPA's goal; made up of different people that disagree on values, is geared towards making the universe a better place. Many of the individuals within the OPA feel oppressed by the UN and MCR and would like to get out of the situation. Others feel as if they need to make the UN and MCR pay.
